Privacy Policy for Memories

Last updated: April 11, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Memories ("we", "our", or "app") handles your personal information and data. We value your trust and strive to be transparent about our privacy practices.

Information Collection

Memories accesses your photo library to display photos and videos from previous years on the current date. All processing of your photos and videos happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your photos, videos, or any metadata associated with them on our servers.

Photo Library Access

Memories requires permission to access your photo library. This access is used solely to:

  • Identify photos and videos from previous years on the current date
  • Display these memories within the app
  • Cache images locally on your device for improved performance

You can revoke this permission at any time through your device's settings.

Data Storage

Memories stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cached images to improve loading performance
  • Basic app preferences and settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use analytics tools to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and is limited to:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Crash reports

Third-Party Services

Memories does not use third-party services that collect your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
